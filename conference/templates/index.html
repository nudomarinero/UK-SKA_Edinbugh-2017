Science Community Workshop

The meeting will be held at the Royal Observatory of Edinburgh on 7th September 2017. It will run from around 11:00am to 17:00pm, and include both talks and extensive discussion time. You can find directions to the ROE in: location

Registration

Developments towards the Square Kilometre Array (SKA) Observatory continue with pace. The on-going cost control project, the planning and development of Regional Processing Centres, and the UK Radio Astronomy Review instigated by STFC's Science Board, all have potentially significant implications for UK scientists aiming to exploit the transformational capabilities of the SKA. This meeting will provide an opportunity for the UK community to keep up-to-date with developments, review the UK's strategic priorities, and discuss how best to achieve these.
